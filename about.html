About me

My name is J. Shamsul Bahri. I am a full stack Web Developer based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. I enjoy making website for both fun and profit. I build hand-crafted, mobile-responsive websites with HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, PHP and other delicious web technologies.

I founded and co-founded two online web publications, better known as ‘webzines’ – jiboneus.com and wirawanweb.com. I am still heavily involved in both of them.

I have been called both an ‘Apple Fanboy’ and a ‘Linux Geek’. I have no idea how that came to be. Recently I had openly confessed my love for the new Microsoft Windows Metro/Modern Interface. This only adds more to the confusion.
